About Me.

Growing up as a third culture kid in a multicultural city like Dubai, I believe my upbringing has instilled a sense of social consciousness in me. It exposed me to perspectives that were beyond mere nationality and gave me an idea about the global cohesiveness that ties mankind together.

For a longtime, my approach to learning has revolved around obtaining a more hands-on experience as opposed to the one-size fits all nature of traditional learning. Hence, I spend alot of time working on projects that bring together my interests and the principles of engineering.

While I'm not programming or finding system exploits, I can be found tinkering around with my Arduino or taking a deepdive into astronomy and politics. We are amidst the digital revolution, for better or for worse, and I hope to contribute to our larger mission by aiding the efforts to build more efficient, robust and secure systems.
