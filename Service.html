Role of Service

Ever since I was a child, I have been enamored with stories of how technology has brought forth a revolution in the world. Engineers have always been at the forefront of making impossible things a reality, it is this drive to make a change that pushes me through the brighest of days and darkest of hours.

Having been bitten by the sustainability bug early on, I spent my high school volunteering at a Dubai based NGO called ‘Green Hope UAE’ who organized environmental campaigns.

Over the course of my time at A&M, I have been involved with the Engineers for a Sustainable World (ESW) where my group designed a sustainability demo showcasing energy consumption discrepencies. Also being an two-term officer at IEEE-TAMU, we organized volunteering & scholarship opportunities for members.

This semester I intend to get more involved with organizations like the 12th Can to reinforce my committment to service as a lifestyle. A life spent not in service of others is a life wasted in my opinion.
